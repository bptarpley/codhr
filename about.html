About

It’s our great pleasure to announce that the Center of Digital Humanities Research has migrated to the TAMU Libraries, and is currently housed within the Scholarly Communications and Data Services. We’ve been hard at work ensuring the continuity of our projects and services using new Libraries affordances. Our website is in its infancy, so check back with us in the coming months for more information as the center evolves. In the meantime, should you have any questions, feel free to reach out at codhr@tamu.edu.
